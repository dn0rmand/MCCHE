Motion

Basic Motion
1) We will use motion against a man to man defense.  The ball handler will yell out, "MOTION" to initiate the play

2) The wings will set screens for the post players

3) Screens are set.  Only then should post players utilize the screens.  The post players should try to keep the defenders guessing as to where they are going.

4) The post players utilize the screens

5) 4 and 5 try to get open on the wings.  2 and 3 try to establish position in the post.
1 will pass to either 4 or 5.

6) Suppose 1 passes to 5.  Now the players closest to the ball screen away from the ball. If the pass had gone to 4 instead, 1 would screen for 5 and 2 would screen for 3.

7) Screen away

8) Utilize the screens "over the top"

9) 5 must be patient waiting for the screens to free up players. 2 is the best option if he is open for a layup.  4 should curl towards the basket if his defender is following him.  Otherwise, he go above the three point line if his defender goes under the screen.

10) If 2 isn't open for a layup, 2 should still try to seal his man for a post entry pass.

11) The pass should be made to the post if he has established position.  This is especially true if we have a mismatch at that position.

12) After the catch is made, a double team may come.  5 should drift to the corner.  If 4's man double teams, he should immedidately cut to the basket.  1 should position himself for as a final option to reset.

13) Now there are at least three options from the post.  To make a post move, to kick out to the corner or to hit the cutter.
